Congratulations on setting out to hike the Pacific Crest Trail. We recognize that even hikers with the best intentions and the highest level of planning may run into obstacles on the trail. Our goal is to help match you with people in the community who are excited and willing to offer help in the form of a place to stay, a warm meal, transportation, or general funding. If you are a hiker who could use a hand, please register below.

For general information about the Pacific Crest Trail, please visit the Pacific Crest Trail Association site.
